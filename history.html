2016


A humble garage soon became home to students from Walnut, Diamond Bar, and Troy High School seeking an environment for STEM inspiration. On March 2, 2016, we competed in the 2016 FRC San Diego Regional, winning the Rookie Inspiration Award.

2017-2019

In our second, third, and fourth seasons, Kenneth and Willam passed the team onto a younger captain, Oscar Chou. And with this new leadership, our team found new heights. Following a team re-brand and becoming a regional quarter-finalist and Engineering Inspiration Award winner in 2018 and 2019, respectively, we were invited to FRC World Championships in Houston.

2020-2021

The operations department handles the logistics of our outreach events, such as the venue, amount of supplies, etc, including finding volunteers. The department also communicates extensively with the finance department to ensure sufficient funds for each event.
